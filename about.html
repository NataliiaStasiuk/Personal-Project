About Me

The Story of Little Ukrainian Potato

I was born and raised in the small town of the West of Ukraine. In order to get a better education I left home and went to boarding school at the age of ten. My senior year of high shcool I spent on the exchange program in the USA, where I lived with a host family and went to American school for ten months. As a result, being for so long on my own, far away from home, have made me very independent and responsible.

I consider myself to be a very outgoing and friendly person, which makes me a great team member! My greatest personality trait is being a fast learner and do my job with integrity and efficiency. I always put the needs of others before my own and approach any task with great responsibility and enthusiasm. I also enjoy communicating with people and being of use to others.

Fun facts about me: I love unicorns and wearing old-fashioned clothing. My favorite color is red. I have a slight obsession with horror and black-and-white movies. I make pancakes when I am frustrated.

My favorite book is "The Thousand Splendid Suns". I have quite a bit of teaching experience. I equally love cats and dogs. I find video games fascinating but cannot play them. I love to drink plain black coffee because it's delicious.
